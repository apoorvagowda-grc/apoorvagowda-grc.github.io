Introduction

Rewind

Rewind is a digital magazine dedicated to celebrating 2000s and 2010s pop culture. We focus on the era’s iconic fashion trends, teen TV shows, early internet culture (MySpace, Tumblr, YouTube beginnings), music (pop-punk, Y2K pop, early 2010s EDM), and tech (iPod, Blackberry, LimeWire). We cater to Gen Z and Millennials who want to relive the highs of a simpler, more expressive digital time.
